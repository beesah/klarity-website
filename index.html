Industry Applications

Built for every industry

Klarity adapts to the unique data challenges of your industry — delivering clarity wherever your business operates.

Retail & Commerce

Track sales performance, customer behaviour, and inventory levels in real time across every store location. Identify top-performing products, spot demand shifts early, and reduce stockouts before they cost you revenue.

Finance & Banking

Detect fraud patterns and suspicious transactions in real time before damage occurs. Automate SAMA compliance reports, monitor portfolio risk across accounts, and surface early-warning signals that protect your institution and customers.

Real Estate

Centralise property data across your entire portfolio — occupancy rates, lease expiries, maintenance costs, and revenue per unit — all in one live dashboard. Make faster investment decisions backed by AI-generated market trend analysis.

Healthcare

Unify patient records, bed occupancy, and clinical workflows into a single operational view. Automate Ministry of Health compliance reporting, flag capacity bottlenecks before they escalate, and improve outcomes through data-driven resource allocation.

Manufacturing

Monitor every production line, machine, and shift in real time with IoT-connected data streams. Predict equipment failures before they cause downtime, track yield efficiency across facilities, and cut waste through AI-powered anomaly detection.

Logistics & Supply Chain

Get end-to-end visibility across your entire supply chain — from warehouse to last-mile delivery. Track shipment status, flag route delays in real time, optimise stock reorder points, and reduce fulfilment costs with predictive demand intelligence.

Food & Hospitality

Monitor kitchen throughput, table turnover, and service times across every branch in one dashboard. Ensure hygiene and operational compliance, identify peak-hour staffing gaps, and maximise guest satisfaction scores with real-time performance alerts.
