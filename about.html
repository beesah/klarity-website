About Klarity

We believe businesses deserve clarity.

Klarity was built for one reason — too many decisions in Saudi Arabia are still made on gut feel, because the data exists but no one can act on it fast enough. We're here to fix that.

Our Story

Built in the Kingdom, for the Kingdom

Klarity was founded by a team of engineers and operators who spent years inside Saudi enterprises watching the same problem repeat itself — data locked in spreadsheets, reports that arrived too late, and decisions made without the full picture.

We built Klarity to close that gap. A platform that takes your existing cameras, data streams, and operational feeds — and turns them into a live intelligence layer that anyone in your organisation can act on, in real time, in Arabic or English.

We are headquartered in the Eastern Province and are proud to be a Saudi-first company supporting Vision 2030's ambition to build a data-driven economy.

Our Values

What drives us

Every product decision, every customer interaction, and every line of code is guided by three principles.

Clarity over complexity

We strip away noise. Every feature we ship must make something simpler — not more impressive. If a customer needs a manual to use it, we haven't done our job.

Trust is earned, not assumed

Your data is your business. We treat it with the same care as our own. We don't sell it, we don't expose it, and we build security in from the first line of code — not as an afterthought.

Saudi businesses first

We don't adapt global templates for the Saudi market. We build for Saudi businesses from the ground up — the culture, the language, the regulatory environment, and the ambition of Vision 2030.
