Privacy Policy

How we collect, use, and protect your information.

Last updated: May 2026

1. Who We Are

Klarity ("we", "our", "us") is a video intelligence and data analytics platform helping businesses make faster, more informed operational decisions. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information collected through our website (klaritysa.com) and services.

2. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

  • Contact information — name, email address, company name, and phone number submitted via our contact or demo request form.
  • Usage data — pages visited, time spent on site, browser type, and device type, collected via standard analytics tools.
  • Communications — any messages or enquiries you send to us directly.

We do not collect payment information directly on this website.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Respond to demo requests and enquiries
  • Communicate with prospective and existing clients
  • Improve our website and services
  • Send relevant product updates or industry information (you may opt out at any time)

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties.

4. Cookies and Analytics

Our website may use cookies and similar tracking technologies to understand how visitors interact with our content. You can disable cookies through your browser settings, though some features of the site may not function as intended.

5. Data Storage and Security

Your information is stored securely and we take reasonable technical and organisational measures to protect it against unauthorised access, loss, or misuse. We retain personal data only as long as necessary for the purposes outlined in this policy.

6. Third-Party Services

We may use third-party services (such as email platforms or analytics tools) that process data on our behalf. These services are bound by their own privacy policies and we take care to use only reputable providers.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request access to the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction or deletion of your data
  • Opt out of marketing communications at any time

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at hello@klaritysa.com.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will revise the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of the site after changes constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.

9. Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please reach out via our contact page or email us at hello@klaritysa.com.
